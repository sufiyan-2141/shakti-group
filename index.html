Welcome to Shakti Group Of Companies, your premier destination for high-quality bike spare parts and Ceat tyres. With a rich history and a steadfast commitment to excellence, we are the trusted partner for retailers seeking reliable distribution of spare parts for leading motorcycle brands and top-grade tyres

At Shakti Group, we specialize in distributing a wide range of motorcycle spare parts, including:

Honda: Our Honda spare parts collection includes everything from engine components to body parts, catering to the diverse needs of Honda bike enthusiasts.

TVS: We offer a comprehensive range of TVS spare parts, meticulously sourced to guarantee reliability and compatibility.

In addition to our spare parts distribution, we proudly operate a state-of-the-art Ceat tyre distribution warehouse. Our extensive inventory of Ceat tyres covers various models and specifications, meeting the demands of both casual riders and professional users
